# E-commerce-Sales-Revenue-Analysis
E- Commerce Sales & Revenue Analysis Dashboard
Data Analytics Project
• Imported and modeled e-commerce sales data from CSV files into Power BI for analysis and visualization.
• Built interactive dashboards to track key metrics including Total Revenue ($757K), Total Orders (3,660), and Total Customers
(3,660).
• Analyzed revenue and discount patterns across product categories and discount ranges to identify high-performing segments.
• Created month-wise revenue trend analysis and payment method distribution to support business decision-making.
• Delivered actionable insights through data storytelling and stakeholder-friendly visualizations.
